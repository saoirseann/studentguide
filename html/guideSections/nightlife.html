Nightlife

Limelight is well known for its nightclub and the fact it's also a live music venue. Its been staging rock & indie shows since the 1980s. This nightclub offers a variety of musical tastes from its three seperate nightclub facilities. We have limelight 1 the main area of the club for our pop and club music, limelight 2 for those more interested in their classic rock and heavy metal abd katies bar joining the two for a more mixed and chilled atmosphere. During the week the club operates a comedy club and student club nights where they host special events, special guests and various DJs. There's also a list of drink promotions on offer as well! The venue now caters to 900 music lovers and comprises of two venues, a bar and a rooftop terrace!

17 Ormeau Ave, Belfast BT2 8HD

£5 admission fees on Mondays, Tuesdays, Thursdays, Friday and Saturdays!

If you're looking for a high energy night Thompsons Garage is the place for you! It originally opened in 1994 as Thompsons Grarage. It was Belfast's first purpose built club aimed at promoting the many genres of dance music on a weekly basis. central location in the city along with the heavy remix music give the club an electric atmosphere. Additionally, the club can hold a good capacity and will often allow entry up to a late time as well as staying open later than some other areas. Thompsons offer compettitve drink promotions alongside a number of Facebook copetitions for giveaways and free entry.

The Hatfield is home to one of Belfast city's oldest bars. The "Hatfield House" is a traditional Irish Bar, with original fixtures, ornate ceilings and lavish craftsmanship carried out by the same craftsmen who worked on HMS Titanic. It's now over 145 years old and is one of the few bars in Belfast city to escape modernisation, and with live entertainment every night, is one of the city's busiest bars. So grab a beer or a cocktail in The Copperplate, our new downstairs space which breathes atmosphere. Check out our great food specials or our 2 for £6 cocktail offers on a Friday! The Hatfield has something for all ages: for craic, atmosphere, live music, great DJs, fresh food, cocktails, big screen sports and roof garden for those sunny days!

130 Ormeau Rd, Belfast BT7 2EB
