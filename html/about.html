The Lost Student Guide is a website targeted at new students made by students. We want to help those who've just moved to Belfast to study and don't know their surroundings very well. We also understand the fact that starting university can be very daunting so we provide a list of mental and physical health services for you on our Help page.

We bring everything you'll need to know plus more on our website, so you hopefully won't be feeling lost for much longer!
